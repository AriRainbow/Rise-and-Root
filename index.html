We envision a world where every young person is empowered to explore wild spaces, connect deeply with nature, and grow into their most authentic selves. In these open and inclusive spaces, they build meaningful skills, discover inner strength, and thrive in community.

Rooted in care, courage, and resilience, they rise with a sense of belonging — prepared to face the world with confidence, curiosity, and connection.

Rise & Root Collective provides free, inclusive, nature-based education for youth ages 6–17, creating space for all to learn, grow, and thrive outdoors. Through hands-on exploration and community-rooted learning, we nurture meaningful connections to land, self, and one another.

Our mission is to uplift and empower young people — especially those often excluded from traditional programs — to grow confident, grounded, and resilient.

Growing Roots for a Stronger Tomorrow

From seed to canopy, we nurture connections between people and the planet.
